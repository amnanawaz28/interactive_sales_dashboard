# Sales & Budget Performance Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview

This interactive dashboard was developed as a **semester project** to analyze sales performance, budget attainment, customer demographics, product profitability, and territory-wise revenue. The dashboard provides actionable insights for business decision-making using historical data (2016–2019).

## 🎯 Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

| Metric               | Value     |
|----------------------|-----------|
| Total Revenue        | $29.36M   |
| Total Profit         | $12.08M   |
| Profit Margin %      | 41.15%    |
| Total Orders         | 28K       |
| Budget Attainment    | 98.38%    |

## 📊 Dashboard Pages

1. **Sales Overview**  
   - Monthly revenue trend  
   - Revenue by product category (Bikes, Accessories, Clothing)  
   - Category sales mix  
   - Top 10 & Bottom 10 products by revenue  

2. **Budget vs Actual Analysis**  
   - Budget attainment %  
   - Actual vs budget by category  
   - Cumulative revenue vs budget over time  
   - Yearly comparison of total revenue vs budget  

3. **Customer Analysis**  
   - Revenue by gender (Male: 49.54%, Female: 50.46%)  
   - Revenue by marital status (Married: 51.73%, Single: 48.27%)  
   - Revenue by occupation, income bracket, and cars owned  

4. **Product Analysis**  
   - Year filter (2016–2019)  
   - Revenue contribution by product category  

5. **Territory Analysis**  
   - Revenue by country (Australia, Canada, France, Germany, UK, US)  
   - Revenue by Australian regions (Victoria, Queensland, NSW, etc.)  
   - Budget attainment % by region and group  

## 🛠 Tools Used

- **Power BI** – Data modeling, DAX calculations, interactive visuals  
- **Excel** – Data cleaning and preparation  

## 📁 Data Source

Sales data from 2016 to 2019, including product, customer, territory, and budget tables.

## 🔍 Key Insights

- **Bikes** is the highest-grossing product category.  
- Budget attainment is very close to target (98.38%).  
- Customer demographics are well-balanced between male/female and single/married.  
- Australia (particularly Victoria and NSW) leads in regional revenue.

## 🚀 How to Use

1. Download the `.pbix` file (Power BI desktop file).  
2. Open with Power BI Desktop (free version available).  
3. Use slicers to filter by year, category, or region.  
4. Hover over charts for detailed tooltips.
